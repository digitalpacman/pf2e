 <p><b>Senses</b> <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/character-creation/#Perception">Perception</a> +10; <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/bestiary/#Darkvision">darkvision</a></p> 
 <p><b>Languages</b> Common, Necril</p> 
 <p><b>Skills</b> <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Acrobatics_Dex">Acrobatics</a> +10, <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Athletics_Str">Athletics</a> +8, <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Deception_Cha">Deception</a> +6, <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Intimidation_Cha">Intimidation</a> +6, <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Nature_Wis">Nature</a> +8, <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Stealth_Dex">Stealth</a> +12, <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Survival_Wis">Survival</a> +8</p> 
 <p><b>Str</b> +2, <b>Dex</b> +4, <b>Con</b> +1, <b>Int</b> +0, <b>Wis</b> +2, <b>Cha</b> +0</p> 
 <p><b>Items</b> composite longbow (20 arrows), hatchet (2), leather armor AC 21 (22 against prey; see Hunt Prey); <b>Fort</b> +9, <b>Ref</b> +12, <b>Will</b> +10; +1 status to all saves vs. death effects</p> 
 <p><b>HP</b> 56; <b>Immunities</b> effects that would transform their body or soul to an undead; <b>Resistances</b> negative energy 2</p> 
 <hr> 
 <p><b>Speed</b> 25 feet</p> 
 <p><b>Melee</b> [one-action] hatchet +12 (agile, sweep), <b>Damage</b> 1d6+5 slashing</p> 
 <p><b>Ranged</b> [one-action] composite longbow +14 (deadly 1d10, propulsive, range increment 100 feet, reload 0, volley 30 feet), <b>Damage</b> 1d8+4 piercing</p> 
 <p><b>Ranged</b> [one-action] hatchet +14 (agile, sweep, thrown 10 feet), <b>Damage</b> 1d6+5 slashing</p> 

<div class="special-abilities">
<p><b>Ghost Hunter</b> The duskwalker&#8217;s weapons have the benefits of the ghost touch property Rune on attacks against incorporeal undead.</p> 
<p><b>Hunt Prey</b> [one-action] The duskwalker designates as its prey a single creature that it can see and hear or that it is Tracking. It gains a +2 circumstance bonus to <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/character-creation/#Perception">Perception</a> checks when it Seeks its prey and a +2 circumstance bonus to <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Survival_Wis">Survival</a> checks when it Tracks its prey. It also ignores the penalty for making ranged attacks within its second range increment against its prey. Lastly, it gains a +2 circumstance bonus to <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Deception_Cha">Deception</a> checks, <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Intimidation_Cha">Intimidation</a> checks, and <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Stealth_Dex">Stealth</a> checks against its prey and to any check to <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Recall_Knowledge_Untrained">Recall Knowledge</a> about its prey, as well as a +1 circumstance bonus to AC against its prey&#8217;s attacks. The duskwalker can have only one prey designated at a time.</p> 
<p><b>Hunted Shot</b> [one-action] <b>Frequency</b> Once per round; <b>Effect</b> The duskwalker makes two longbow Strikes against its hunted prey. If both Strikes hit, combine their damage for the purpose of resistances and weaknesses.</p>
</div>

<p class="header">About</p>
 <p>Duskwalkers are infused with the same energies as psychopomps. These ashen scions are reborn on the Material Plane to serve as guardians of the cycle of life and death.</p>
