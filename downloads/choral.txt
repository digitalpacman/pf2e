<p class="header">About</p>
<p>Choral angels are incredible singers who fill the halls of Nirvana with pious chants and sacred hymns. They form from the souls of talented bards and other performers. While they shy away from conflict, they occasionally brave the mortal realm to deliver good omens and auspicious messages. Choral angels often serve the goddess Goddess of Art, Beauty, Love, and Music, but they can also serve other good deities and empyreal lords.</p>
