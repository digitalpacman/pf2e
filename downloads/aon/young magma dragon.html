Magma Dragon Spellcasters

To make a magma dragon spellcaster, remove the dragon’s Draconic Frenzy and draconic momentum abilities and give it the following spells. You can swap out any number of these with other primal spells, provided you keep the same number of spells for each level. You might also want to increase the dragon’s Wisdom or Charisma modifier by 1 or 2 to reflect its mastery of magic.

Dragon, Magma

Source Pathfinder #148: Fires of the Haunted City pg. 80
Magma dragons are considered unpredictable and brash by other dragons. They lair within volcanically active mountains and throughout the steaming, stony borderlands between the Plane of Fire and the Plane of Earth.

Primal Dragons

Magma dragons belong to a caste of dragons known as primal dragons—massive scaled beings that hail from the Elemental Planes and the Plane of Shadow. Other primal dragons include power-hungry brine dragons from the Plane of Water, whimsical cloud dragons from the Plane of Air, haughty crystal dragons from the Plane of Earth, and sadistic umbral dragons from the Shadow Plane.
