                                        <h1>Leukodaemon (Pestilence Daemon)</h1>
                    <div class="page-center">
                        <div class="article-content">
                             <h4 class="monster">Leukodaemon <span class="monster-level">Creature 9</span></h4> 
 <p class="traits"><span class="alignment">NE</span> <span class="size">Large</span> <span class="trait">Daemon</span> <span class="trait">Fiend</span></p> 
 <p><b>Senses</b> <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/character-creation/#Perception">Perception</a> +20; <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/bestiary/#Darkvision">darkvision</a>, plaguesense 60 feet</p> 
 <p><b>Languages</b> Common, Daemonic; telepathy 100 feet</p> 
 <p><b>Skills</b> <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Acrobatics_Dex">Acrobatics</a> +18, <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Intimidation_Cha">Intimidation</a> +18, <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Medicine_Wis">Medicine</a> +20, <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Religion_Wis">Religion</a> +20, <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Stealth_Dex">Stealth</a> +18, <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/skills/#Survival_Wis">Survival</a> +16</p> 
 <p><b>Str</b> +6, <b>Dex</b> +5, <b>Con</b> +1, <b>Int</b> +3, <b>Wis</b> +5, <b>Cha</b> +3</p> 
 <p><b>Items</b> +1 Striking composite longbow (50 arrows)</p> 

<div class="special-abilities">
<p><b>Plaguesense</b> A leukodaemon senses any creature with a disease, and it knows the type and current stage of all diseases carried by any creature within range.</p>
</div>

 <hr> 
 <p><b>AC</b> 28; <b>Fort</b> +15, <b>Ref</b> +21, <b>Will</b> +19; +1 status to all saves vs. magic</p> 
 <p><b>HP</b> 155; <b>Immunities</b> death effects, disease; <b>Weaknesses</b> good 10</p> 

<div class="special-abilities">
<p><b>Infectious Aura</b> (aura, disease) 30 feet. Leukodaemons radiate infection. All creatures within 30 feet of a leukodaemon take a -2 status penalty to saves against disease effects. If a creature within range contracts or progresses a disease, all adjacent creatures are exposed to the same disease, at the same DC.</p>
</div>

 <hr> 
 <p><b>Speed</b> 25 feet, fly 40 feet</p> 
 <p><b>Melee</b> [one-action] jaws +21 (disease, evil, magical, reach 10 feet), <b>Damage</b> 2d12+9 piercing plus 1d6 evil and daemonic pestilence</p> 
 <p><b>Melee</b> [one-action] claw +21 (agile, disease, evil, magical, reach 10 feet), <b>Damage</b> 2d8+9 slashing plus 1d6 evil and daemonic pestilence</p> 
 <p><b>Ranged</b> [one-action] composite longbow +21 (deadly 1d10, disease, evil, magical, propulsive, range increment 100 feet, reload 0, volley 30 feet), <b>Damage</b> 2d8+9 piercing plus 1d6 evil and Daemonic Pestilence</p> 

<p><b>Divine Innate Spells</b> DC 26; <b>5th</b> <a class="spell" href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/spell/dimension-door/">dimension door</a>; <b>4th</b> <a class="spell" href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/spell/dimension-door/">dimension door</a> (at will), <a class="spell" href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/spell/dispel-magic/">dispel magic</a> (×2); <b>1st</b> <a class="spell" href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/spell/detect-alignment/">detect alignment</a> (at will; good only)</p> 

<div class="special-abilities">
<p><b>Breath Weapon</b> [two-actions] (evocation, divine) The leukodaemon exhales a cloud of corpse-bloated, biting black flies in a 20-foot cone. Creatures within the cone take 4d8 piercing damage (DC 28 basic <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/character-creation/#Reflex">Reflex</a> save). A creature that fails the save becomes sickened 1 (or sickened 2 on a critical failure).</p> 
<p><b>Daemonic Pestilence</b> (disease) The leukodaemon can telepathically communicate with the afflicted creature at any distance on the same plane; <b>Saving Throw DC</b> 28 <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/character-creation/#Fortitude">Fortitude</a>; <b>Stage 1</b> carrier (1 day); <b>Stage 2</b> drained 1 (1 day); <b>Stage 3</b> drained 2 (1 day); Stage 4 drained 2 (1 day); Stage 5 drained 3 (1 week); Stage 6 dead</p> 
<p><b>Quicken Pestilence</b> [one-action] (divine, manipulate, necromancy) The leukodaemon coaxes a disease into full bloom. It chooses a target in its aura of pestilence that&#8217;s currently affected by a disease. That creature must attempt a <a href="https://pf2.d20pfsrd.com/rules/character-creation/#Fortitude">Fortitude</a> save against the disease as if the interval for the disease&#8217;s current stage had passed.</p> 
</div>

<p class="header">About</p>
<p>These skull-headed, vulture-winged daemons are harbingers of pestilence and servants of their patron Horseman, Apollyon. Manifestations of evil souls who perished from disease in life, leukodaemons work tirelessly to spread disease across all the worlds of the multiverse. More than any other daemon, leukodaemons cooperate with each other and those who conjure them to mortal worlds to spread sickness and plague, eschewing personal glory for end results.</p>
