<p class="header">About</p>
<p>Hamadryads rule over an entire forest, or a portion of an incredibly large forest, leading and protecting all dryads within. Hamadryads often have strange relationships with powerful and deadly fey, working together in a dualistic way despite their differences, with the hamadryad representing nature&#8217;s wonders and the other fey representing nature&#8217;s wrath.</p>
